Smart Lantern XP-1

Take a step out of darkness with the brightest light

Why Is It Special

Easy to use

The simple operations of the device are bound to make the device favorable for all age groups

Environment friendly

The device has the potential to run on solar energy making it an ideal device for energy conservation

Multifunctional

The device works as a lamp, torch and a music system which makes it perfect for use for every situation

Features

Multipurpose lamp

The device consists of 5W lamp and a 1W torch which helps it in functioning as a lamp and a torch as and when required.

IOT-based smart light

The lantern allows for every setting to be controlled by mobile phone or voice assistants, like Google Home and Alexa ensuring ease of use.

Omnidirectional light diffuser

The Lantern provides ample light intensity to brighten a hall and the omnidirectional light diffuser ensures that a larger area is light up in all the directions.

Music at fingertips

The device has a USB port that can be used to charge the phone and can be connected to 3W 2-inch speaker. It can be used to listen to music through USB, AUX cable, or Bluetooth FM radio.

Energy efficient light

The device has a solar charging panel and requires only 3-4 hours of sunlight to charge up proving to be an energy efficient source of light.

Long lasting battery life

The battery of the device has the capacity to last for three days at an average use of 3-4 hours each day ensuring maximum output at a low consumption of energy.
